# Template: Malicious Communications – Burgess Principle Request for Human Review

Use this when you are the victim of malicious communications (threats, harassment, offensive messages, false accusations, etc.) and want the police, platform, or relevant authority to conduct a proper individual review of your specific case rather than dismissing it through automated filters or generic responses.

---

**Subject:** Request for Human Review – Malicious Communications Against Me

Dear [Police Force / Platform Trust & Safety / Relevant Authority] Team,

I am writing to report malicious communications I have received, which I believe constitute an offence under the Malicious Communications Act 1988 (or equivalent legislation).

I respectfully ask for written confirmation on this single binary question in line with the Burgess Principle:

**“Was a human member of the team able to personally review the specific facts of my specific case before any decision was reached?”**

The specific facts of my case are:

• I have received [describe the communications briefly and factually, e.g. “repeated threatening messages via [platform], sent on [dates], containing [specific nature of content]”].
• These messages have caused me [specific impact, e.g. distress, fear for my safety, reputational harm].
• The sender is [known/unknown] and the content appears to be intended to cause distress or anxiety.

I am not asking for special treatment. I am simply requesting that a real human officer or reviewer with judgment has personally examined the specific facts of my case, rather than relying solely on automated filters, keyword checks, or generic triage processes.

I would be grateful for a response within 7 days confirming:

1. That a named human reviewer has personally reviewed my specific case.  
2. The outcome of that review and any action taken (e.g. investigation opened, content removed, warning issued, etc.).

Thank you for treating me as an individual who deserves protection from malicious communications.

Yours sincerely,
