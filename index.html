The Core

The Binary Test

Every finding under the Burgess Principle resolves to one of two outcomes. Before the institution acted on you — was a human there?

SOVEREIGN

A human mind was individually applied to the specific facts. The action was considered. It may still be wrong — but it was human.

NULL

No individual human review took place. The instrument carries no lawful authority from inception. The system processed — nobody decided.
