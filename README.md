## Advanced Applications

The Burgess Principle is designed to work at every scale — from everyday bureaucracy to high-stakes domains where automated decisions have serious consequences.

- **[Media & Libel](templates/MEDIA_AND_LIBEL.md)** — For inaccurate, unfair, or repeatedly negative media coverage. Forces outlets to confirm meaningful human review of the specific facts.
- **[Music Copyright](templates/MUSIC_COPYRIGHT_WITH_BURGESS.md)** — For wrongful Content ID claims, blocked monetisation, or royalty disputes. Ensures a human reviewer examines the specific track and claim.
- **[Laws, Warfare & Autonomous Systems](LAWS_AND_AUTONOMOUS_SYSTEMS.md)** — For drone targeting, Lethal Autonomous Weapon Systems (LAWS), and other military/AI contexts where individual human oversight is critical.

These advanced templates maintain the same calm, respectful tone while adapting the core question to more complex environments.

Contributions in these areas are welcome, provided they preserve the human-first spirit of the project. See [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md) for guidelines.
