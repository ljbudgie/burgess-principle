# Template: Platform Policy / Subscription Change Impact – Burgess Principle Request for Human Review

Use this when a platform suddenly changes its terms, removes features, restricts access, or reduces the value of a paid subscription without clear individual explanation or review of your specific usage.

---

**Subject:** Request for Human Review – Impact of Recent Policy / Subscription Change on My Account

Dear [Platform Name] Customer Experience / Policy / Support Team,

I am writing as a paying subscriber regarding a recent policy or feature change that has significantly affected my use of the service.

I respectfully ask for written confirmation on this single binary question in line with the Burgess Principle:

**“Was a human member of the team able to personally review the specific facts of my specific account and usage before this change was applied to me?”**

The specific facts of my case are:

• I have been an active [subscription tier / paid plan] user for [duration].
• I relied on [specific feature or capability, e.g. third-party integrations, certain tools, reach, or access level] as part of my regular workflow.
• The recent change [brief neutral description, e.g. “removal of third-party tool support” or “reduction in visibility/reach”] has directly reduced the value and functionality I receive from my subscription.
• No individual notification or explanation was provided about how this affects my specific usage.

I am not disputing the platform’s right to update its policies. I am requesting that a real human reviewer with judgment personally examines the specific facts of my account — including my subscription history, usage patterns, and the practical impact of the change — rather than applying a blanket policy through automation.

I would be grateful for a response within 7 days confirming:

1. That a named human reviewer has personally reviewed my specific case.  
2. The outcome of that review and any action taken (e.g. exception granted, alternative offered, compensation for lost value, or clear explanation of the decision).

Thank you for treating me as an individual subscriber rather than just another account affected by a policy update.

Yours sincerely,  
[Your Full Name / Handle]  
[Account email or subscription ID if relevant]
