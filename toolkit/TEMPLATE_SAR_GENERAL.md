# General Subject Access Request

A professional, copy-paste ready Subject Access Request template that works for any institution. Based on Article 15 UK GDPR.

Send this by email where possible. Record the date you send it — the organisation has **30 calendar days** to respond.

---

## Template

```
Subject: Subject Access Request — [YOUR NAME]

Dear Data Protection Officer,

[INSTITUTION NAME]

I am writing to make a Subject Access Request under Article 15 of the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

Please provide the following information in relation to my account/reference [ACCOUNT/REFERENCE NUMBER]:

1. PERSONAL DATA
   A copy of all personal data you hold about me, in any format, including
   but not limited to account records, correspondence, internal notes,
   call recordings, and file notes.

2. AUTOMATED DECISION-MAKING
   Under Article 22 UK GDPR, please confirm whether any decision affecting
   me — including any decision to take enforcement action, apply charges,
   issue a default, or refer my account to a third party — was made by an
   automated system without meaningful human intervention.

   If automated processing was used, please provide:
   (a) The logic involved in the automated processing (Article 15(1)(h))
   (b) The significance and envisaged consequences of that processing
   (c) The identity of any human who meaningfully reviewed my specific
       case before the decision was made

3. HUMAN REVIEW
   Please provide the name and role of any individual who personally
   reviewed my specific case in connection with the following action:
   [DESCRIPTION OF ACTION TAKEN AGAINST YOU]

4. THIRD-PARTY SHARING
   Please provide details of any third parties with whom my personal data
   has been shared, including:
   (a) The identity of each third party
   (b) The categories of data shared
   (c) The lawful basis relied upon for each disclosure

5. RETENTION PERIODS
   Please confirm the retention period for each category of personal data
   you hold about me, or the criteria used to determine the retention
   period.

6. REASONABLE ADJUSTMENTS (if applicable)
   I am a disabled person within the meaning of section 6 of the Equality
   Act 2010. My condition is [YOUR DISABILITY IF APPLICABLE].

   Under section 20 of the Equality Act 2010, I require the following
   reasonable adjustment: all future communication with me must be
   conducted in writing (email or letter) only.

   This adjustment takes effect immediately upon receipt of this notice.
   (Remove this section if not applicable to your circumstances.)

IDENTITY VERIFICATION
I confirm my identity as follows:
- Full name: [YOUR NAME]
- Date of birth: [YOUR DOB]
- Address: [YOUR ADDRESS]
- Email: [YOUR EMAIL]
- Account/Reference: [ACCOUNT/REFERENCE NUMBER]

I am happy to provide further identification if reasonably required.

DEADLINE
Under Article 12(3) UK GDPR, you are required to respond to this request
within 30 calendar days of receipt. If you are unable to comply within
that period, you must inform me of the reason for the delay and the
expected date of response.

Yours faithfully,

[YOUR NAME]
[YOUR ADDRESS]
[YOUR EMAIL]
```

---

## Placeholder Fields

| Placeholder | What to enter |
|-------------|---------------|
| `[YOUR NAME]` | Your full legal name |
| `[YOUR ADDRESS]` | Your current postal address |
| `[YOUR DOB]` | Your date of birth |
| `[YOUR EMAIL]` | Your email address |
| `[INSTITUTION NAME]` | The name of the organisation you are writing to |
| `[ACCOUNT/REFERENCE NUMBER]` | Any account number, reference, or customer ID they use for you |
| `[DESCRIPTION OF ACTION TAKEN AGAINST YOU]` | A short description of what they did — e.g. "the enforcement visit on 15 March 2026" or "the default registered on my credit file" |
| `[YOUR DISABILITY IF APPLICABLE]` | A brief description of your condition — e.g. "autism spectrum condition" or "chronic pain condition". Remove the entire reasonable adjustments section if not applicable. |

---

## After Sending

- Record the date you sent the request.
- If they do not respond within 30 days, see the [Ombudsman/Regulator Complaint](TEMPLATE_OMBUDSMAN_REFERRAL.md) template.
- If their response does not answer your specific questions, follow up in writing referencing the original request date and the unanswered points.
