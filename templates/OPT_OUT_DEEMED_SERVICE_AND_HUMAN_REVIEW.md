# Opt-Out of Deemed Service and Request for Certified Human Review

**Template Purpose**  
This template is used when you wish to formally withdraw consent to deemed service under the Civil Procedure Rules and request that any future action or correspondence involves specific human review of your individual circumstances.

---

**Date:** [Insert today's date]

**To:**  
[Insert full name or "Company Secretary" / "Legal Department" of the organisation]  
[Organisation Name]  
[Full postal address]  
[Email address if known and previously used]

**Subject:** Formal Notice – Withdrawal of Consent to Deemed Service and Request for Certified Human Review  
**Reference:** [Your account number or reference, if applicable]

Dear [Sir/Madam or named individual if known],

I am writing regarding any current or potential proceedings, enforcement action, or correspondence concerning my account or circumstances under reference [insert reference if appropriate].

1. **Withdrawal of Consent to Deemed Service**  
I formally withdraw any consent, implied or otherwise, to the service of documents by methods that would trigger **deemed service** under CPR 6.14 (claim forms) or CPR 6.26 (other documents).  

I do not consent to service by first-class post, DX, or any electronic method that relies on a deemed date of service rather than actual receipt and acknowledgment.

2. **Request for Alternative Service**  
Please serve all future documents by a method that provides clear proof of actual delivery and receipt. I am happy to discuss a suitable alternative method that ensures I receive the documents directly.

3. **Request for Certified Human Review**  
Before any further step is taken in relation to my situation, I kindly request a written statement from a named member of your team who has **personally reviewed the specific facts of my specific circumstances**.  

This statement should confirm:  
- That they have examined my individual situation, including any relevant personal circumstances or needs;  
- That they accept responsibility for the decision or action being considered;  
- That the matter has received specific human attention rather than automated or bulk processing.

This request is made in support of dealing with the matter fairly and proportionately, in line with the overriding objective under CPR 1.1.

4. **Record of This Communication**  
A cryptographic commitment (SHA-256 hash with Ed25519 signature) of this notice has been created and stored securely in my personal verifiable record as evidence of this request. I am happy to provide a selective disclosure proof if it would assist.

I would be grateful for your prompt response, ideally from the named individual who has carried out the review. Please address all future correspondence to the contact details at the top of this letter.

Thank you for your attention to this matter. I remain open to resolving this constructively once the requested human review has taken place.

Yours sincerely,  

[Your Full Name]
