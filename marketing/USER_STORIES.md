# User Stories  
**— Real Experiences with the Burgess Principle**

Hi 👋

This page is a gentle, growing collection of real experiences from people who have used the Burgess Principle.

We keep stories anonymous unless someone specifically asks to be named. The goal is simple: to show that you are not alone, and that one respectful question can sometimes make a real difference.

### Share Your Story
If the Burgess Principle helped you (even in a small way), feel free to open an issue on the repository with your experience.  
We only include stories that feel kind and hopeful — no drama needed.

### Early Stories

**Story 1 – Energy Bill & CCJ**  
“I received a big energy bill and a CCJ for a period before I even moved in. I was panicking. I used the GENERAL_DISPUTE_WITH_BURGESS_PRINCIPLE template and simply asked if a human had looked at my actual move-in date. Scottish Power replied within two weeks with the correct meter readings and withdrew the CCJ. I didn’t have to go to court. For the first time I felt like someone actually listened.”

**Story 2 – Equality Act & Email-Only Request**  
“I’m autistic and phone calls cause me severe anxiety. I sent the Equality Act starter pack template to my local council asking for email-only contact plus human review of my housing application. They agreed to email-only within days and a case worker personally reviewed my file. The adjustment made everything less overwhelming. I finally felt seen.”

**Story 3 – Simple DSAR Follow-Up**  
“I used the DSAR_WITH_BURGESS_PRINCIPLE template with my bank after an automated credit decision. I politely asked if a real person had reviewed my specific circumstances. They replied with a named reviewer and corrected an error on my file. It was such a relief to know a human had actually looked.”
