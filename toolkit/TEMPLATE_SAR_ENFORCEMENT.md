# Enforcement Visit Subject Access Request

A forensic SAR template for challenging any enforcement visit — bailiffs, debt collectors, energy enforcement agents, or water disconnection agents. This template requests the authorisation chain, bodycam footage, vulnerability flags, and confirmation of whether the referral was automated or individually reviewed.

Based on the actual methodology used in the British Gas and Wave/iC&R cases.

Send this by email where possible. Record the date — the organisation has **30 calendar days** to respond.

---

## Template

```
Subject: Subject Access Request — Enforcement Visit [DATE OF VISIT]

Dear Data Protection Officer,

[INSTITUTION NAME]

I am writing to make a Subject Access Request under Article 15 of the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

This request relates specifically to the enforcement visit to my property
at [PROPERTY ADDRESS] on [DATE OF VISIT], and to all decisions leading to
that visit.

Please provide the following information:

1. PERSONAL DATA
   A copy of all personal data you hold about me, in any format, including
   account records, correspondence, internal notes, call recordings, and
   file notes.

2. AUTHORISATION OF THE VISIT
   (a) The instruction, referral, or authorisation document that led to
       the enforcement visit to my property
   (b) The name and role of the individual within your organisation who
       reviewed my account and authorised the referral to enforcement
   (c) Confirmation of whether the referral to enforcement was made by
       an automated system or was individually reviewed by a named person
   (d) If individually reviewed, the date of that review and any notes or
       records from it
   (e) If automated, the criteria or rules used by the automated system
       to trigger the referral

3. ENFORCEMENT AGENT RECORDS
   (a) The enforcement agent's report or notes from the visit on
       [DATE OF VISIT]
   (b) Any bodycam or audio recording footage from the visit
   (c) The name and employer of the enforcement agent who attended
   (d) Any instructions given to the agent regarding my specific account

4. VULNERABILITY AND ACCOUNT FLAGS
   (a) Any vulnerability flags, Priority Services Register (PSR) entries,
       or disability-related notes on my account at the time the
       enforcement referral was made
   (b) Confirmation of whether these flags were visible to the person or
       system that authorised the enforcement referral
   (c) Confirmation of whether these flags were communicated to the
       enforcement agent before the visit

5. AUTOMATED DECISION-MAKING
   Under Article 22 UK GDPR, please confirm whether any decision in the
   chain — from account management to enforcement referral to agent
   instruction — was made by an automated system without meaningful human
   intervention.

   If automated processing was used at any stage, please provide:
   (a) The logic involved in the automated processing (Article 15(1)(h))
   (b) The significance and envisaged consequences of that processing
   (c) The identity of any human who meaningfully reviewed my specific
       case before the decision was made at each stage

6. HUMAN REVIEW
   Please provide the name and role of any individual who personally
   reviewed my specific account before:
   (a) The decision was made to refer my account to enforcement
   (b) The enforcement agent was instructed to attend my property

7. THIRD-PARTY SHARING
   Please provide details of any third parties with whom my personal data
   has been shared in connection with this enforcement action, including:
   (a) The identity of each third party (e.g. enforcement agency, debt
       collector, field agent company)
   (b) The categories of data shared
   (c) The lawful basis relied upon for each disclosure

8. RETENTION PERIODS
   Please confirm the retention period for each category of personal data
   you hold about me, or the criteria used to determine the retention
   period.

9. LITIGATION HOLD
   I put you on notice that a complaint and/or legal proceedings may
   follow in connection with this enforcement action. Under section 173
   of the Data Protection Act 2018, you must not alter, destroy, or
   conceal any personal data or documents relevant to this matter.

   Please confirm that a litigation hold has been placed on all records
   relating to my account and this enforcement action.

IDENTITY VERIFICATION
I confirm my identity as follows:
- Full name: [YOUR NAME]
- Date of birth: [YOUR DOB]
- Address: [YOUR ADDRESS]
- Email: [YOUR EMAIL]
- Account/Reference: [ACCOUNT/REFERENCE NUMBER]

I am happy to provide further identification if reasonably required.

DEADLINE
Under Article 12(3) UK GDPR, you are required to respond to this request
within 30 calendar days of receipt. If you are unable to comply within
that period, you must inform me of the reason for the delay and the
expected date of response.

Yours faithfully,

[YOUR NAME]
[YOUR ADDRESS]
[YOUR EMAIL]
```

## Placeholder Fields

| Placeholder | What to enter |
|-------------|---------------|
| `[YOUR NAME]` | Your full legal name |
| `[YOUR ADDRESS]` | Your current postal address |
| `[YOUR DOB]` | Your date of birth |
| `[YOUR EMAIL]` | Your email address |
| `[INSTITUTION NAME]` | The organisation responsible for the enforcement visit (e.g. the energy company, council, or creditor — not necessarily the agent who attended) |
| `[ACCOUNT/REFERENCE NUMBER]` | Your account or customer reference number |
| `[PROPERTY ADDRESS]` | The address the enforcement agent visited |
| `[DATE OF VISIT]` | The date the enforcement agent attended your property |

## When to Use This Template

Use this template when an enforcement agent has visited your property and you want to establish:

- **Who authorised the visit** — was there a named individual who reviewed your account, or was it an automated referral?
- **What they knew about your circumstances** — were vulnerability flags visible and considered?
- **What the agent did** — bodycam footage and agent reports provide the factual record.
- **Whether the entire chain was automated** — from account management through to the knock on your door.

This applies to:
- Energy enforcement agents (prepayment meter installation, disconnection)
- Bailiffs and enforcement agents (council tax, parking, court orders)
- Debt collection agents attending in person
- Water disconnection or meter installation agents

---

## After Sending

- Record the date you sent the request.
- If they do not respond within 30 days, see the [Ombudsman/Regulator Complaint](TEMPLATE_OMBUDSMAN_REFERRAL.md) template.
- The litigation hold assertion puts them on notice to preserve all evidence. If they destroy records after receiving this, that is itself a serious matter.
- Keep every response — it forms part of your evidential record.
