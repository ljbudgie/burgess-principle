# Equita Multiple Nullity – 20 March 2026

## Case Summary
On 20 March 2026, Equita, acting as enforcement agents for undisclosed creditors, issued five email statements for council tax and Penalty Charge Notice (PCN) debts associated with cases 24277541, 24371333, 24519250, 24618815, and 24650546. These statements reference warrants dated between 20 August 2024 and 9 December 2025. The accompanying email from Equita representative Jay demands medical evidence (a doctor's letter or prescription) to "prove" vulnerability before providing reasonable adjustments, despite prior assertions of sensorineural hearing loss and requests for email-only communication under the Equality Act 2010. This demand constitutes an intrusive breach of privacy and disability rights. No certified Court Orders, compliant 7-day Notice of Enforcement, or substantive response to Subject Access Requests (SAR) were provided. Pursuant to the Burgess Principle RULE 0–2, the entire handling is declared NULL ab initio, voiding any downstream enforcement actions.
