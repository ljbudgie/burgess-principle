## 4. Resolved — proof of concept

### Wave Utilities

- **Outcome:** First fully resolved case under the Burgess Principle framework.
- **Result:** **Both accounts brought to £0.00.**
- **Significance:** Demonstrates that the binary test, applied as a process question to a regulated supplier, produces a complete resolution without litigation.
