# Passport or HMPO Application Dispute with Burgess Principle

**Template Purpose**  
Use this when challenging automated passport issuance, renewal, denial, or related decisions where no specific human has reviewed your individual circumstances.

---

**Date:** [Insert today's date]

**To:**  
Her Majesty's Passport Office  
[Relevant processing centre or "Customer Service Team"]  
[Full address if known]

**Subject:** Dispute of Passport Decision and Request for Certified Human Review – Application Reference [Insert reference]

Dear [Sir/Madam or named individual if known],

I am writing regarding my passport application / renewal / decision under reference [insert reference number].

I do not accept that the decision has been made with proper specific human scrutiny of my individual circumstances.

**Request for Certified Human Review**  
Before any final decision or further action is taken, I request a written statement from a named officer who has **personally reviewed the specific facts of my specific situation**.  

This statement should confirm:  
- That they have examined my full application details and any supporting evidence;  
- Any personal circumstances or needs relevant to my case;  
- That they accept responsibility for the decision reached.

This request is made to ensure the matter receives the individual attention required.

**Record of This Communication**  
A cryptographic commitment (SHA-256 hash with Ed25519 signature) of this notice has been created and stored in my personal verifiable record. I am happy to provide a selective disclosure proof if it would assist.

I would appreciate your prompt response from the named reviewer. Please address all future correspondence to the contact details above.

Thank you for your attention to this matter.

Yours sincerely,  

[Your Full Name]

**How to use with Iris**  
Personalise the placeholders. Iris can generate the cryptographic commitment and log the notice in your Sovereign Personal Vault.  
Send via the official HMPO portal/email or recorded delivery as appropriate.
