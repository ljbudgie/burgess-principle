# Template: Royalty or Attribution Dispute – Burgess Principle Request for Human Review

Use this when a label, publisher, or collecting society has incorrectly allocated royalties or failed to credit your contribution.

---

**Subject:** Request for Human Review – Royalty / Attribution Dispute for “[Track Title]”

Dear [Label / Publisher / Society Name] Legal / Royalty Team,

I am writing regarding the royalty allocation and/or attribution for my contribution to “[Track Title]”.

I respectfully ask for written confirmation on this single binary question in line with the Burgess Principle:

**“Was a human member of the team able to personally review the specific facts of my specific case before any decision was reached?”**

The specific facts of my case are:

• I contributed [describe your exact role – songwriting, production, performance, etc.].  
• My contribution entitles me to [specific royalty percentage or credit].  
• The current allocation/credit does not reflect my actual contribution.

I am simply requesting that a real human reviewer has personally examined the specific facts of my contribution and the dispute, rather than relying on automated systems or default templates.

I would be grateful for a response within 7 days confirming:

1. That a named human reviewer has reviewed my specific case.  
2. Any correction to royalty allocation or attribution that will be made.

Thank you for treating my creative contribution as an individual piece of work.
