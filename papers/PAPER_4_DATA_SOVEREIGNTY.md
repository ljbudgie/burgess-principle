## Abstract

The SOVEREIGN/NULL binary extends to personal data: processing without individual authorisation is NULL — and UK GDPR provides the reclamation toolkit.

Forensic DSARs across institutions reverse data-value flow. The Equality Act 2010 multiplies remedies for disabled subjects. Extensions reach medical-device governance (hearing aids) and technology licensing.

## Part I: The Original Sin – Data as Uncompensated Currency

Modern digital economy extracts personal data without compensation. UK GDPR rights (access, objection, erasure) exist as a shield — yet are treated as compliance checkboxes.

## Part IX: Medical Device Extension – Sensory Sovereignty
