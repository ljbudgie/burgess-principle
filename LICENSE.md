## What the Mark Certifies

Use of the Burgess Certified mark confirms that the holder:

1. **Applies the binary test** — has implemented a process by which the question *"Was a human member of the team able to personally review the specific facts of this person's situation?"* is asked and answered for decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects on individuals.
