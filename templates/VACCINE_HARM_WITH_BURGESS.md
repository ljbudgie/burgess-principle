# Template: Vaccine Harm / Adverse Reaction – Burgess Principle Request for Human Review

Use this template when seeking proper individual review of a vaccine-related adverse reaction, injury, or denial of support. It asks for meaningful human oversight rather than automated or group-based responses.

---

**Subject:** Request for Human Review – My Specific Case of Vaccine Adverse Reaction

Dear [Relevant Authority / Healthcare Provider / Compensation Scheme / Regulator] Team,

I am writing regarding my personal experience following [vaccine name / batch if known] administered on [date].

I respectfully ask for written confirmation on this single binary question in line with the Burgess Principle:

**“Was a human member of the team able to personally review the specific facts of my specific case before any decision was reached?”**

The specific facts of my case are:

• I received the [vaccine name] on [date].  
• I subsequently experienced [brief, factual description of symptoms / diagnosis / impact on daily life].  
• This has resulted in [specific harms: medical costs, lost earnings, ongoing disability, etc.].  
• My request for [support / compensation / further investigation / record correction] has so far been handled through automated or standard processes without individual review.

I am not making a general statement about vaccines. I am simply requesting that a real human reviewer with medical and administrative judgment has personally examined the specific facts of my case, rather than relying solely on checklists, statistical thresholds, or automated systems.

I would be grateful for a response within 14 days confirming:

1. That a named human reviewer has personally reviewed my specific case.  
2. The outcome of that review and any action taken (acknowledgement of the adverse event, provision of support, correction of records, referral for further assessment, etc.).

Thank you for treating me as an individual rather than just another report in the system.

Yours sincerely,  
[Your Full Name]  
[Date of birth / NHS number / Vaccination record reference if relevant]
