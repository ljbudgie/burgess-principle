# Citizen's Legal Toolkit

This directory contains ready-to-use tools for deploying the Burgess Principle framework against any institution.

No legal training is required. No fee is charged. Everything here is free under the MIT licence.

---

## Start Here

The Burgess Principle asks one question of every institution that makes a decision affecting you:

> *"Was a human member of the team able to personally review the specific facts of my situation?"*

If the answer is no — or if they can't tell you — this toolkit gives you the tools to document that, escalate it, and build a record that matters.

## How to Use

1. **Choose your tool** — pick the one that matches your situation
2. **Copy the template** — each one contains a clearly marked letter or email you can copy directly
3. **Fill in the placeholders** — replace every `[PLACEHOLDER]` with your own details
4. **Send by email where possible** — everything is then timestamped and documented
5. **Note the date you sent it** — the 30-day clock starts from the date of receipt
6. **If they don't respond** — use the ombudsman referral template to escalate

---

## What Happens Next

Every response you receive — or don't receive — is part of your record. The Burgess Principle works because it creates a documented audit trail that institutions cannot quietly make disappear. Keep every email. Note every date. The record is everything.
