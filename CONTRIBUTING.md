# Contributing to the Burgess Principle

Thank you for wanting to help!

**Before you open a Pull Request:**
- Open an Issue or Discussion first to discuss the change.
- All changes must strictly align with the core binary question and the registered UK Certification Mark UK00004343685.
- The official doctrine (main branch) is the certified version. Experimental ideas belong in forks.

We welcome:
- New NULL findings with full evidence (add to LIVE_AUDIT_LOG.md)
- Improvements to templates that make them clearer/stronger
- Bug fixes in toolkit scripts
- Typos, formatting, or clarity edits

We do **not** merge:
- Changes that weaken the human-judicial-mind test
- Pseudolaw-style additions or anything that could be seen as frivolous
- Large rewrites without prior discussion

Only changes that maintain the integrity of the Principle will be merged into the official repo.
