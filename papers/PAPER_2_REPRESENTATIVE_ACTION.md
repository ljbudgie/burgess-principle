The originating warrant (unsigned, metadata-assigned names) is systemic, not isolated. HMCTS centralised systems enable widespread bulk issuance without scrutiny. Foreign-owned energy suppliers benefit commercially from void instruments.

Redress is due — and must be delivered efficiently via representative action.
