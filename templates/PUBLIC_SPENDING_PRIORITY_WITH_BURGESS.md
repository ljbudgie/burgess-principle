# Template: Public Spending Priority / Budget Decision – Burgess Principle Request for Human Review

Use this when a public body (council, government department, or authority) makes a spending decision that appears misprioritised, and you want them to explain the human review and reasoning behind it.

---

**Subject:** Request for Human Review – Public Spending Priority Decision

Dear [Public Body Name, e.g. Council / Government Department] Finance / Cabinet / Communications Team,

I am writing as a resident / taxpayer regarding a public spending decision that raises questions about value for money and prioritisation of limited funds.

I respectfully ask for written confirmation on this single binary question in line with the Burgess Principle:

**“Was a human member of the team able to personally review the specific facts, costs, and competing priorities of this spending decision before it was approved?”**

The specific facts of this case are:

• The public body approved expenditure on [brief neutral description of the item/project, e.g. “a specific symbolic installation”].
• This decision was made in the context of [optional neutral context, e.g. “ongoing pressures on essential local services” or leave blank if you prefer to keep it very general].
• As a resident/taxpayer, I am concerned about transparency and whether the decision properly balanced competing needs.

I am not challenging the right of the public body to make spending decisions. I am simply requesting that a real human decision-maker with oversight personally reviews the specific facts of this case — including cost, justification, alternatives considered, and impact on other public services — rather than treating it as a standard budget line or automated approval.

I would be grateful for a response within 14 days confirming:

1. That a named officer or elected representative has personally reviewed this specific spending decision.  
2. The outcome of that review, including the full business case or prioritisation reasoning.

Thank you for treating residents and taxpayers as individuals who deserve transparency on how public funds are spent.

Yours sincerely,  
[Your Full Name]  
[Your Address / Ward / Constituency if relevant]  
[Contact email]
