# Template: DMCA Counter-Notice – Burgess Principle Request for Human Review

Use this when you have received a DMCA takedown notice that you believe is wrongful (e.g. fair use, misidentification, or bad-faith claim).

---

**Subject:** DMCA Counter-Notice – Request for Human Review of My Content “[Title]”

Dear [Platform Name] Copyright / Legal Team,

I am writing in response to the DMCA takedown notice received on [date] regarding my content “[Title or Description]” (URL: [link]).

I respectfully ask for written confirmation on this single binary question in line with the Burgess Principle:

**“Was a human member of the team able to personally review the specific facts of my specific case before any decision was reached?”**

The specific facts of my case are:

• My content qualifies as fair use / is original / does not infringe the claimant’s copyright [brief factual explanation].  
• The takedown notice appears to be mistaken or in bad faith.  
• The removal is causing harm to my [channel / account / livelihood].

I am not disputing the DMCA process itself. I am simply requesting that a real human reviewer has personally examined the specific facts of my content and the claim, rather than relying on automated systems or the claimant’s statement alone.

I would be grateful for a response within 7 days confirming:

1. That a named human reviewer has reviewed my specific case.  
2. Whether the content will be restored and any further action taken.

Thank you for treating my work as an individual creative piece rather than just another automated takedown.

Yours sincerely,  
[Your Full Name]
