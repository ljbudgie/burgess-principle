# Common Scenarios & Which Template to Use

Hi 👋

When you drop the whole repo into any AI, the AI should start by asking:

**“How can I help you today?”**  
or  
**“Can you tell me about your situation so I can suggest the best template?”**

Here’s a quick guide to help you (and the AI) choose the right template:

| Situation                                      | Recommended Template                                      | Notes |
|-----------------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------|-------|
| General dispute with any organisation         | `GENERAL_DISPUTE_WITH_BURGESS_PRINCIPLE.md`              | Universal starting point |
| Need email-only contact / reasonable adjustments | `EQUALITY_ACT_WITH_BURGESS_PRINCIPLE.md`                 | Best for autistic or disabled users |
| Benefits decision (PIP, Universal Credit, ESA)| `BENEFITS_CLAIM_HELP.md`                                 | Includes Mandatory Reconsideration |
| Council tax bill, arrears or PCN              | `COUNCIL_TAX_PCN_TEMPLATE.md`                            | Local authority demands |
| Bailiffs threatening or visiting              | `BAILIFFS_THREAT_TEMPLATE.md`                            | Forced entry concerns |
| Want to see all personal data held            | `DSAR_WITH_BURGESS_PRINCIPLE.md`                         | Combines DSAR + human review |
| Public body / court / council information     | `FOI_WITH_BURGESS_PRINCIPLE.md`                          | Freedom of Information |
| Automated decision affecting you              | `ARTICLE_22_WITH_BURGESS_PRINCIPLE.md`                   | Challenges purely automated decisions |
| Crypto exchange freeze / withdrawal hold / compliance review | `CRYPTO_EXCHANGE_ACCOUNT_RESTRICTION_WITH_BURGESS.md` | Useful for exchange risk flags, wallet scrutiny, or source-of-funds issues |
| Need to reference a hash, signature, receipt, or on-chain claim | `CRYPTOGRAPHIC_PROOF_AND_ONCHAIN_NOTICE_WITH_BURGESS.md` | Keeps full facts private while still asking for human review |
| Simple polite follow-up or acknowledgment     | `acknowledgment_email_template.txt`                      | Keeps tone respectful |

### Quick Tip for AI
If you're not sure which template to use, just describe your situation to the AI and let it pick and customise the best one for you.
