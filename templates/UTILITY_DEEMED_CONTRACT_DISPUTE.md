I do not accept that a valid contract exists in my case, as no specific human reviewer has personally examined the facts of my individual situation, including any vulnerabilities or prior communications.

**Request for Certified Human Review**  
Before any further action, billing, or enforcement is taken, I request a written statement from a named member of your team who has **personally reviewed the specific facts of my specific circumstances**.  

This statement should confirm:  
- That they have examined my individual account history, any stated vulnerabilities or needs, and the basis for imposing the deemed contract;  
- That they accept responsibility for the decision to treat my supply as subject to a deemed contract;  
- That the matter has received specific human attention rather than automated or bulk processing.

This request aligns with dealing with the matter fairly and proportionately.

**Record of This Communication**  
A cryptographic commitment (SHA-256 hash with Ed25519 signature) of this notice has been created and stored in my personal verifiable record. I am happy to provide a selective disclosure proof if helpful.

I would appreciate your prompt response from the named individual who has carried out the review. Please address all future correspondence to the contact details above.

Thank you for your attention. I remain open to constructive resolution once the requested human review has taken place.

Yours sincerely,  

[Your Full Name]
