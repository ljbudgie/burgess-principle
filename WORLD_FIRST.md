## The Declaration

The Burgess Principle is a world first.

It is the first open-source accountability framework for meaningful human involvement in automated decision-making to achieve all of the following simultaneously:

---

**1. Registered as a certification mark**

UK Certification Mark UK00004343685, Classes 41, 42, 45, filed at the UK Intellectual Property Office. USPTO filing in progress. The mark certifies that a specific institutional process includes meaningful human involvement — the statutory standard now enshrined in Articles 22A–22D of the UK GDPR under the Data (Use and Access) Act 2025.

No other open-source accountability framework for automated decision-making holds a registered certification mark anywhere in the world.

---

**2. Deployed in live proceedings across 26+ institutions**

Not proposed. Not piloted. Deployed — across energy enforcement, local government, courts, medical devices, financial services, data protection, equality, and AI. Findings include a written admission from a Justices' Legal Adviser confirming 536,139 warrants processed en bloc without individual judicial review.

One resolved case. One SOVEREIGN finding. Wave Utilities: both accounts to £0.00, £795.14 in fees removed, in a single exchange with a named human being.

---

**3. Independently converged with statute**

The framework arrived at the standard of meaningful human involvement before the Data (Use and Access) Act 2025 brought Articles 22A–22D into force on 5 February 2026. The convergence was independent — built from personal necessity, not from the statute. Parliament and the framework reached the same answer from different directions.

---

**4. Integrated into a major open-source AI agent**

PR #12265 submitted to NousResearch/hermes-agent (99.1k stars), proposing the Burgess Principle as a default-enabled human-impact awareness feature. Additive — no existing code modified.

---

**5. Published with a complete cryptographic enforcement layer**

SHA-256 commitment generation. AES-256-GCM local encryption. Ed25519-signed receipts. Merkle-rooted tamper-evident ledger. Optional on-chain claim fingerprinting with no personal data on-chain. All local-first. All open-source. All free.

---

**6. Originated by a single individual, built on a phone**

Lewis James Burgess. Deaf. Darlington. A barber. No law firm. No university. No institution. An iPhone and 600+ timestamped commits.

The system assumed he would not read the warrant. He read it. The system assumed he would not find the defect. He found it. The system assumed he would not build the framework. He built it.

---

**7. Named after its originator in US federal case law**

*Burgess v. Merit Systems Protection Board*, 758 F.2d 641 (Fed. Cir. 1985) established what US administrative law calls Burgess notice: before a government agency processes a claim affecting an individual, a specific human being must personally review the specific facts of that specific case.

The framework and the federal precedent share a name. They share a standard. They arrived at it independently, forty years apart.
