# Note on Apple Privacy Features

Hi 👋

The Burgess Principle really appreciates Apple Inc.’s strong commitment to user privacy and data sovereignty.

We especially admire how Apple has led the way with features such as:

- End-to-end encryption (iMessage and FaceTime)
- On-device intelligence and processing
- App Tracking Transparency
- Private Relay and Advanced Data Protection

These efforts help people feel more in control of their own data, which aligns beautifully with the simple idea at the heart of the Burgess Principle: that every person should be seen and treated as an individual, not just a data point.

We’re grateful for any company that makes real privacy tools available to ordinary users.

Take care,
