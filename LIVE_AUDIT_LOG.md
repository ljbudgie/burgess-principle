## Taxonomy Key

Each institution is scored across five dimensions, each 0–4. Total is out of 20.

| Band | Score | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **NULL** | 0–5 | No meaningful human consideration. Full escalation indicated. |
| **Marginal** | 6–10 | Minimal compliance only. Formal challenge and DSAR pressure warranted. |
| **Partial sovereign** | 11–15 | Some human consideration present. Remedy may be achievable without escalation. |
| **SOVEREIGN** | 16–20 | Full human consideration demonstrated and verifiable. Positive precedent. |

**Dimensions scored:**
- **D1 — Response to the question** (0–4): Did the institution directly answer the Burgess Principle binary question?
- **D2 — Evidence of human review** (0–4): Is there documented evidence a named or identifiable human examined the specific facts?
- **D3 — Timeliness** (0–4): Did they respond within their statutory obligation?
- **D4 — Remedy offered** (0–4): Was a proportionate remedy offered and delivered where harm was found?
- **D5 — Recurrence / systemic change** (0–4): Has the same behaviour recurred, or is there evidence of systemic change?

- Standard rebuttal: ask again for a direct **YES** or **NO** answer and, if **YES**, the **name and role** of the human reviewer.
