## Summary

On 25 May 2025, E.ON Next executed forced entry at a residential property under warrant Application 11160-544079. The warrant was unsigned on its face, and Birmingham Magistrates' Court later confirmed that energy warrants were processed in CSV batches with dip sampling and en bloc granting. E.ON's own field notes recorded that the customer was deaf, yet enforcement still proceeded without reasonable adjustment. This case shows the Burgess Principle at the highest-stakes end of enforcement: entry to a home without any named person individually reviewing the specific facts.
