# Template: Politician Contradiction / U-Turn – Burgess Principle Request for Human Review

Use this when a politician or government official has publicly said one thing (e.g. in a speech, manifesto, or interview) but their actions or policies have done the opposite, and you want them (or their office/department) to personally review the specific impact on you or your community.

---

**Subject:** Request for Human Review – Contradiction Between [Politician’s Name]’s Public Statements and Actions Affecting Me / My Community

Dear [Politician’s Name / Constituency Office / Relevant Government Department] Team,

I am writing as a constituent / affected individual regarding a clear contradiction between [Politician’s Name]’s public statements and the actions or policies that have followed.

I respectfully ask for written confirmation on this single binary question in line with the Burgess Principle:

**“Was a human member of the team able to personally review the specific facts of my specific case before any decision or policy implementation was reached?”**

The specific facts of my case are:

• On [date / occasion], [Politician’s Name] publicly stated [exact quote or clear summary of what was said, e.g. “Every day seems to bring a new existential risk to British farming… you deserve better than that”].
• However, the subsequent actions / policies have resulted in [specific opposite outcome, e.g. “measures that increase financial pressure on farmers, risk farm closures, or undermine food security”].
• This has directly affected me / my community in the following ways: [brief, factual description of personal or local impact].

I am not seeking a general political debate. I am requesting that a real human reviewer with judgment personally examines the specific facts of this case — the public commitment versus the real-world outcome and its impact on individuals — rather than treating it as standard political rhetoric or automated policy implementation.

I would be grateful for a response within 14 days confirming:

1. That a named human reviewer has personally reviewed my specific case.  
2. The outcome of that review and any action taken (e.g. explanation of the apparent contradiction, reconsideration of the policy’s impact, or steps to mitigate harm to affected individuals).

Thank you for treating me as a real constituent whose life is affected by the gap between words and actions.

Yours sincerely,  
[Your Full Name]  
[Your Constituency / Location]
