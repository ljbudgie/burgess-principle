## What Is the Burgess Principle?

One simple, respectful question:

> **"Was a human member of the team able to personally review the specific facts of my situation?"**

It is a diagnostic tool — not pseudolaw — designed to help ordinary people confirm whether a real person reviewed their individual case before an institution exercised power over them.

- If yes → **SOVEREIGN** (lawful and proper).
- If no → **NULL** (no proper individual authority from the start).

## The SOVEREIGN / NULL Binary

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **SOVEREIGN (1)** | A named human individually reviewed the specific facts and accepted personal responsibility for the decision. |
| **NULL (0)** | No individual human review took place. The action was bulk-processed or automated without meaningful human oversight. |
