Use this for any warrant, forced entry notice, or enforcement action threatening access to your home (utilities, bailiffs, or other authorities) where no specific human has reviewed your individual circumstances.

I am writing in relation to any warrant, notice of entry, or enforcement action concerning my home at [insert address] under reference [insert reference if known].

I require confirmation that a named human member of your team has **personally reviewed the specific facts of my specific situation** before any entry or enforcement proceeds.

**Request for Certified Human Review**  
Please provide a written statement from a named individual who has examined:  
- My individual circumstances and any vulnerabilities or needs;  
- The specific grounds and necessity for entry in my case;  
- That they accept personal responsibility for authorising any warrant or entry.

Absent such certified human review, I must treat the process as lacking proper authority and will record it accordingly.
