# Court Defence or Application Notice with Burgess Principle

**Template Purpose**  
Use this as a base for drafting a defence statement, application notice, or response in court proceedings where you need to assert the need for specific human review (including in representative actions under CPR 19.8).

---

**Date:** [Insert today's date]

**To:**  
[Court Name]  
[Claim Number / Case Reference]  
[Claimant / Opposing Party Name]

**Subject:** Defence / Application in Claim [Insert claim number] – Request for Specific Human Review

Dear Sir/Madam,

I am writing in relation to the above claim or proceedings.

I respectfully request that the court ensure any decision or order in this matter is supported by specific human review of my individual circumstances rather than automated or deemed processes.

**Request for Certified Human Review**  
I ask that the opposing party (or relevant authority) provide a statement from a named individual who has personally reviewed:  
- The specific facts of my case;  
- Any vulnerabilities or personal circumstances relevant to the proceedings;  
- And who accepts responsibility for the position taken.

This is requested in line with the overriding objective under CPR 1.1 to deal with the case justly and at proportionate cost.

**Record of This Communication**  
A cryptographic commitment of this notice has been generated and stored in my personal verifiable ledger for evidentiary purposes.

I would be grateful for the court's consideration and for any directions that ensure proper individual scrutiny of my situation.

Yours sincerely,  

[Your Full Name]
