# Template: Direct Debit Refund / Fraud – Burgess Principle Request for Human Review

Use this when a Direct Debit has been taken fraudulently or incorrectly, the bank has refused or reversed a refund, or you have been pushed to the Financial Ombudsman after the bank investigated itself. It highlights the gap between the advertised Direct Debit Guarantee ("full and immediate refund") and the reality of the process.

---

**Subject:** Request for Human Review – Direct Debit Refund / Fraud on My Account

Dear [Bank / Building Society Name] Complaints / Fraud / Customer Services Team,

I am writing regarding a Direct Debit taken from my account that I believe was fraudulent or unauthorised, and the subsequent handling of my refund request.

I respectfully ask for written confirmation on this single binary question in line with the Burgess Principle:

**“Was a human member of the team able to personally review the specific facts of my specific case before any decision was reached?”**

The specific facts of my case are:

• On [date], a Direct Debit of £[amount] was taken from my account by [company/organisation name or reference].
• I did not authorise this payment / it was fraudulent / I cancelled the authority beforehand.
• I requested a refund under the Direct Debit Guarantee, which promises a “full and immediate refund” with no conditions.
• The bank has [refused the refund / investigated itself and rejected my claim / reversed the refund / pushed me to the Financial Ombudsman].

I am not challenging the Direct Debit scheme itself. I am requesting that a real human reviewer with judgment has personally examined the specific facts of my case — including the original transaction, my cancellation/refusal, the bank’s investigation process, and the gap between the advertised Guarantee and the actual outcome — rather than relying solely on automated systems, internal checklists, or self-investigation by the bank.

I would be grateful for a response within 7 days confirming:

1. That a named human reviewer has personally reviewed my specific case.  
2. The outcome of that review and any action taken (e.g. full refund issued, charges waived, escalation to an independent reviewer, etc.).

Thank you for treating me as an individual customer rather than just another Direct Debit case in the system.

Yours sincerely,  
[Your Full Name]  
[Account number / Sort code if relevant]
