# Template: DEI (Diversity, Equity, Inclusion) – Burgess Principle Request for Human Review

Use this when seeking individual consideration in situations involving diversity, equity, inclusion, or protected characteristics (e.g. disability, race, gender, religion, etc.), where decisions appear to have been made through automated, quota-based, or group-level processes without looking at your specific circumstances.

---

**Subject:** Request for Human Review – My Specific Case under DEI / Equality Policies

Dear [Organisation / HR / DEI Team / Admissions / Funding Body] Team,

I am writing regarding the decision concerning my application / case / situation [brief description, e.g. job application, reasonable adjustment request, promotion, funding application, etc.].

I respectfully ask for written confirmation on this single binary question in line with the Burgess Principle:

**“Was a human member of the team able to personally review the specific facts of my specific case before any decision was reached?”**

The specific facts of my case are:

• I have [protected characteristic(s), e.g. bilateral sensorineural hearing loss / other relevant detail].  
• I requested [specific reasonable adjustment or consideration].  
• My individual circumstances include [brief, factual points that make your case unique, e.g. length of service, specific impact on daily functioning, supporting medical evidence, etc.].

I fully support the principles of fairness and equal opportunity. I am simply requesting that a real human reviewer with judgment has personally examined the specific facts of my case, rather than relying solely on automated scoring, quotas, checklists, or group-based criteria.

I would be grateful for a response within 14 days confirming:

1. That a named human reviewer has personally reviewed my specific case.  
2. The outcome of that review and any action taken (e.g. granting of adjustment, reconsideration of decision, etc.).

Thank you for treating me as an individual rather than just another data point in a diversity metric.

Yours sincerely,  
[Your Full Name]
