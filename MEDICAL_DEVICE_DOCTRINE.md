# The Burgess Principle: Medical Device Extension
## Algorithmic Authority Over Sensory Experience

**The Burgess Principle applied to hearing aid fitting architecture.**
*If they didn't individually authorise it, it's void. The frequency world you live in must bear the mark of a human mind.*

## The Extension

The Burgess Principle was developed in enforcement law — a utility warrant issued without individual judicial review, void ab initio. The same binary test applies to any instrument that exercises authority over a citizen.

A hearing aid is such an instrument. It does not enter your home. It enters your head. It governs what frequencies are amplified, suppressed, compressed, or shifted. It determines directional microphone focus. Through automated operating systems, it makes real-time algorithmic decisions about how the wearer perceives their environment — approximately 700 times per second.

The Principle asks: **who individually authorised it?**

## The SOVEREIGN/NULL Test Applied to Hearing Aids

### NULL (0) — Void Ab Initio
- Audiogram entered into manufacturer fitting software
- Proprietary algorithm (e.g. Adaptive Phonak Digital / APD) generates a "first fit" from population-level data
- Automated operating system (e.g. AutoSense OS) takes over real-time sensory decisions
- No Real Ear Measurement (REM) verification performed
- The citizen lives inside a machine-authorised frequency world that no individual human mind ever scrutinised for them

### SOVEREIGN (1) — Valid
- Frequency-specific audiometry individually conducted
- Named clinician individually reviews and prescribes
- Evidence-based fitting formula (NAL-NL2 or DSL v5), not proprietary default
- Real Ear Measurement verification proves output matches prescription in that specific ear
- Operating system parameters individually reviewed and consciously authorised
- The citizen lives inside a human-authorised frequency world

## Five Questions for Hearing Aid Manufacturers

These questions were formally submitted to Sonova Holding AG (parent company of Phonak) on 6 March 2026:

### 1. First Fit Algorithm
When fitting software generates a "first fit" using a proprietary formula, that prescription is algorithmically calculated from population-level data. Is this first fit individually scrutinised by a clinician before deployment, or is it applied as a default?

### 2. Automated Operating System
Operating systems like AutoSense OS make real-time algorithmic decisions about what the wearer hears — gain, frequency response, directional microphone activation, noise reduction, program switching. Who individually authorised the delegation of these continuous decisions over a citizen's sensory experience to a machine-learning algorithm? Was individual informed consent obtained?

### 3. Proprietary vs Evidence-Based
Manufacturer fitting software defaults to proprietary formulas rather than independently validated NAL-NL2 or DSL v5. What is the clinical and ethical justification for defaulting to a formula that serves commercial interests over independently validated standards?

### 4. The Invisible Data Layer
Hearing aids contain firmware defaults, AI-generated recommendations, and signal processing parameters invisible to the wearer. These constitute an invisible data layer governing sensory experience — the same structural problem as invisible magistrates' names in a warrant's metadata layer while the face remains blank.

### 5. The Verification Gap
Published audiological literature acknowledges that Real Ear Measurement — the only step that individually verifies device output in the patient's ear canal — is "the step most often skipped." Manufacturers are therefore aware that their devices are routinely deployed without individual verification. This is the missing signature on the warrant.

## Domain Invariance

The Burgess Principle is domain-invariant. The same binary test that invalidates a bulk-processed warrant invalidates an algorithmically generated hearing aid prescription deployed without individual human scrutiny.

This is not analogy. It is the same structural problem in a different domain. The test is the same. The logic is the same. The remedy is the same: **individual human scrutiny at every layer, or the instrument is void.**
